Core Features

Everything your factory needs,
all in one platform

Packivo covers the full manufacturing cycle — from raw material procurement to final dispatch — with real-time insights at every step.

Raw Material Management

Track Duplex sheets, Kraft rolls, and consumables with GSM, size, and lot tracking. Never run out of stock unexpectedly.

Production Job Scheduling

Plan 3-ply, 5-ply, 7-ply production runs. Track progress in real time, assign operators, and monitor efficiency metrics.

Finished Goods Inventory

Maintain customer-wise product stock. Set low-stock alerts, adjust inventory, and view movement history with full audit trail.

Dispatch & Challan Management

Generate delivery challans and invoices instantly. Track dispatches by vehicle, route, and customer with full logistics history.

Reports & Analytics

Daily production summaries, vendor-wise purchase reports, wastage analysis, and monthly P&L breakdowns — all exportable to Excel.

Role-Based Access & Audit Trail

Assign roles — Admin, Manager, Operator. Every action is logged with timestamps and user details for complete accountability.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! Every plan comes with a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card is required to start. You can upgrade or cancel at any time.

Can I import my existing Excel data?

Absolutely. Packivo supports bulk Excel import for vendors, customers, products, and historical purchases. We also provide import templates to help you format your data correctly.

How many users can I add?

The Starter plan supports up to 5 users, Professional supports up to 20, and Enterprise has unlimited users. You can assign different roles — Admin, Manager, and Operator — with custom permissions.

Is my data secure and backed up?

Yes. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We perform daily automated backups and maintain a 30-day recovery window. Our infrastructure runs on 99.9% uptime SLA.

Do you support GST invoicing?

Yes, Packivo is GST-compliant. You can record purchase invoices with GST details, generate delivery challans with tax breakdowns, and export GST-ready reports for your CA.

Can I use Packivo on mobile?

Packivo is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. You can check stock, record entries, and view reports from your phone or tablet without installing any app.
